Usage Method

Quick Button Usage

After enabling the shortcut button in the extension settings, a convenient AI button will automatically appear when you select text on a webpage. Clicking on this button will quickly bring up the conversation window, making your operations more smooth.

Shortcut Usage

You can use the custom shortcut key to bring up the conversation window regardless of whether text is selected.
